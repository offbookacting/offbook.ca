Off Book

Acting without Distracting.

About

Off Book is an all-in-one tool for performers to organize, rehearse, and publish their performance without ever leaving the app.

A tool to make performers feel like a craftsman.

Features

Easy to Learn, Hard to Master

Off Book is approachable for everyone, yet if you'd like to, there's a comprehensive suite of powerful tools available.

Annotate

Annotate PDFs within the app. Make notes and scribble thoughts on the page for later reference. Full pen support included.

Read

Read out loud your script or any text media. Take in the text you'll be performing before you begin rehearsing so you can approach it like an artist. Also includes the ability to record your own "table read" so that you can listen back to it later.

Rehearse

Rehearse your lines any way that works for you. The app doesn't insist on one method of rehearsal. Rather, it lets you choose what works for you, giving you all the tools you need to perfect your system. Allowing you to feel like performing is your craft. We aim to put every rehearsal method out there inside the app.

Sidebar File System

Collapsible sidebar with easy folder organization. Intuitive, powerful design that keeps everything at your fingertips.

Listen to Audio Files

Within the integrated filesystem, audio files can be viewed and played conveniently. Listen to reference files, previous takes, character dialects. Record audio within the app.

Watch Video Files

Integrated with all other files. View and watch videos seamlessly within the app alongside your scripts and notes.

Local, Standardized Storage

Edit files within our app, on your PC, or on cloud storage—it's up to you. If you ever leave the app or don't have it installed, all the files it creates are standardized and usable in perpetuity. Backup to cloud storage is easy by having Google Drive downloaded locally on your machine.

We Don't Judge How You Use the App

If you want to write scripts within the app for an integrated rehearsal and writing experience, you can. We'd love to discover new ways people use the app. It's a rehearsal tool, but it's also for: stand-up comedy, speeches, wedding speeches, wedding speeches where you don't like the groom, wedding speeches where you don't like the bride, rehearsing a conversation (à la "The Rehearsal") like the conversation where you tell your sister that you don't like her husband, or even drawing a picture of a dog.

Record Self Tape Right Within the App

There will eventually be a clean, simple layout for recording self tapes and choosing takes using a simple UI. Actors told me they often use the editing software with the fewest features even if they're skilled editors in more complex software. My goal is to make it as simple as possible to edit a self tape.

Web Browser

Open any web files in Off Book. Use web-based acting tools (International Dialects of English Archive for accent reference). Publish your self tape within the app. Save web bookmarks for easy access to frequently visited webpages. Download your script from Actors Access, Casting Network, etc. Download scripts from the internet. Files downloaded from the integrated browser will download right into the project folder and go immediately into the files sidebar. Procrastinate, right within the app.
